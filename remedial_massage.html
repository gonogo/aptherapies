Remedial Massage

Remedial massage can help alleviate a variety of conditions and may incorporate similar techniques to those used in a typical sports massage. A remedial massage will, in addition, help you in the following ways:

◊ Reduce stiffness and pain — resulting from long-term problems, such as muscle over-use (RSI) and neck, shoulder and back pain caused by, for example, a sedentary job or long hours driving
◊ Provide relief from chronic conditions — Remedial massage and assisted stretching can help alleviate some of the distressing muscular symptoms associated with conditions such as Multiple Sclerosis and Parkinson's Disease
◊ Improve posture — when poor posture has resulted from a long-term muscle problem
