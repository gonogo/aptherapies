Sports Massage Therapy

In general, there are a number of ways in which sports massage will help you:

◊ Maintain muscle condition — A combination of massage and stretching techniques will help prevent muscles from shortening. This will improve and help maintain flexibility, enable full range of movement and prevent injury
◊ Identify and treat injuries early — An experienced massage therapist may identify small injuries of which the client might not be aware and can provide treatment to prevent more significant injury developing
◊ Aid faster recovery from muscle injuries — Massage will improve blood circulation, thus removing waste products from the muscles and increasing the supply of oxygen and other nutrients necessary for the healing process
◊ Remove and help prevent the formation of scar tissue — promoting healing and preventing further injury from over-use
◊ Promote relaxation — with both physical and psychological benefits
◊ Pre-event and post-event treatment — are aimed at preventing injury and speeding up recovery
