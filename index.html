Sports and Remedial Massage Therapy

◊ For those who play sports, whether at a professional, amateur or recreational level
◊ For other muscular problems and conditions which are not necessarily sports-related

I provide sports and remedial massage for people in all walks of life, whether involved in sports or not. Massage can bring may benefits, including improved flexibility, faster recovery from injury and relief from pain.

I am based in the village of Holt, near Trowbridge, Wiltshire. I see people from many of the surrounding towns — Bradford-on-Avon, Melksham, Chippenham and Trowbridge.
